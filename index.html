What is Meditation?

About

Meditation is a great way to help improve your mental health.

Techniques

Meditation is a great way to help improve your sleep.

Stages

1: awareness of the present moment. 2: subduing the negative mind. 3: mindfulness of the meditation object. 4: dawning awareness. 5: unwavering commitment.

Benefits

Self-Knowledge, Reflective thought, Therapeutic Meditation and Contemplation
